Easy French Dip Sandwiches

Food Information

  • Ingredients

  • olive oil 2 tablespoons
  • shallot A ton
  • all purpose flour A ton
  • steak seasoning A ton
  • roast beef A ton
  • baguette A ton
  • pepper A ton

  • Equipment

  • frying pan
  • whisk
  • tongs
  • bowl
    • Recipe How to
    In a large skillet over medium heat, add olive oil and heat for 1 minute.
    Add shallots and sauté for 2 minutes.
    Add flour and sauté 2 more minutes.Slowly whisk in beef consommé. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to low and simmer.
    Add steak seasoning and pepper.Separate roast beef and place in the au jus sauce until warm. Using tongs, place an equal portion of beef on each section of baguette or rolls. Top with a slice of provolone cheese, if desired.
    Serve with a small bowl of the extra au jus for dipping. Enjoy!
